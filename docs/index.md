### Detailed examples

* [Repository](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1QXs6KyLu9JkMq7UQ-q2TDFPcPIICgoHE?usp=sharing)

### Selected examples for quick start

* [01_ediff_au.nb.pdf](https://drive.google.com/file/d/19C6ClDDepcpNNjl12OduE_d_WpFr0HMd/view?usp=sharing)
  = Jupyter notebook converted to PDF; <br>
  a complete processing of powder electron diffraction pattern,
  including the comparison with theoretically calculated diffractogram.
* [02_ediff_center.nb.pdf](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1KZPi2ck_TWZURUxe3EdLEBM8r7BAlS5g/view?usp=sharing) 
  = Jupyter notebook converted to PDF; <br>
  a demo, how to use the automatic center determination for
  both polycrystalline and monocrystalline electron diffraction patterns.
